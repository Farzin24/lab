About MEA Engineering College

MEA Engineering College, established by the Muslim Educational Association (MEA), is a prominent institution offering quality engineering education. The college aims to provide state-of-the-art facilities and a supportive environment for students to excel academically and professionally.

Departments

MEA Engineering College offers various departments like Computer Science, Civil Engineering, and Electronics, among others. Each department is designed to offer the best possible education and support to its students.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a center of excellence in engineering education and research.

Mission: To foster technical skills and ethical values in students, preparing them to contribute positively to society.

MASTER IN COMPUTER APPLICATION(MCA)

In RIT, Kottayam, the Master of Computer Applications (MCA) course was started in the year 2001. This post graduate course aims to mould professionals to meet the complex demands of the IT industry.
